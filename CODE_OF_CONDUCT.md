# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an open,
welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people;
- respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences;
- giving and accepting constructive feedback; and
- taking responsibility and learning from mistakes.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory or derogatory
comments, public or private abuse, unwanted sexual attention, publishing
private information without permission, and any conduct that would reasonably be
considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards
and may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to behavior they
consider unacceptable. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments,
commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Scope and reporting

This Code applies within project spaces and whenever an individual officially
represents the project. Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project
maintainers through a private GitHub contact. For security vulnerabilities,
follow the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Please include relevant
context and links, and avoid posting sensitive details publicly. Reports will be
reviewed promptly and kept confidential as far as practical.
